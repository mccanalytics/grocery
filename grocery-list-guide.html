Opening the app

The Grocery List app is a single HTML file — there's nothing to install, no account to create, and no internet connection required after the first time you open it.

1
Save the file grocery-list.html somewhere easy to find — your Desktop, Documents folder, or Downloads folder all work great.
2
Double-click the file. It will open in your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox — any of them work).
3
That's it! Your lists, stores, and settings are automatically saved right in the browser. Closing the tab or the browser and coming back later is completely fine — everything will be exactly where you left it.
⚠️
Keep the file safe Your data is stored in the browser, not in the file itself. That means if you open the file in a different browser or on a different computer, you'll start fresh. Use the Backup feature (Section 10) to protect your data.

Setting up your stores

The app lets you save up to 5 different stores, each with its own aisle layout. This is great if you shop at more than one place — for example, your weekly grocery store and a warehouse club like Costco.

💡
When you first open the app You'll start with one store called "My Store." You can rename it and set it up to match wherever you shop most.

Adding a new store

1
Look at the row of colored pill buttons near the top of the screen — those are your store tabs. Find the + Store button at the end and tap it.
2
Pick an icon (emoji) from the grid and type your store name — for example: Meijer, Costco, Aldi.
3
Tap + Add Store. The new store is created with a default set of aisles you can customize.

Renaming or deleting a store

1
Switch to ✏️ Edit mode using the toggle near the top right.
2
Small ✏️ and ✕ buttons will appear on the active store's tab. Tap ✏️ to rename or pick a new icon. Tap ✕ to delete the store (you'll be asked to confirm).
🌿
Pro tip: match your real store layout Set up aisles in the order you actually walk through the store. You'll never backtrack for a forgotten item again.

Setting up aisles

Aisles are the sections of your list — things like "Produce," "Dairy," or "Frozen Foods." Each store has its own set of aisles. You only need to set these up once; after that you just add items.

🔑
You need Edit mode for this Tap the ✏️ Edit button near the top of the screen to switch into Edit mode.

Adding a new aisle

1
In Edit mode, a bar appears at the top that says "New aisle name…"
2
Type the aisle name (e.g. Produce) and tap + Aisle or press Enter.
3
The new aisle appears at the bottom. Repeat for as many aisles as you need.

Reordering aisles

Drag the ⠿ handle on any aisle to drag it to a new position. On mobile or tablet, you can also use the ▲ and ▼ buttons that appear in Edit mode to move aisles up or down one step at a time.

Renaming or deleting an aisle

In Edit mode, each aisle has ✏️ and 🗑️ buttons. Tap ✏️ to rename, 🗑️ to delete (and all its items).

Adding items to your list

You can add items to any aisle in both Shop and Edit mode — you don't have to switch modes just to build your list.

1
Open any aisle by tapping its name (if it's collapsed, tap the › chevron on the right to expand it).
2
At the bottom of each aisle, you'll see an "Add item…" text box and a smaller "Qty" box next to it.
3
Type the item name in the first box (e.g. Apples). Optionally, type a quantity in the Qty box (e.g. 2 lbs, 6, 1 bunch).
4
Tap Add or press Enter. The item appears in the list immediately.
💡
Quantity matters for the Deal Counter If you type 6 in the Qty box and mark the item as a deal item (⭐), it will count as 6 toward your deal — not just 1. See Section 7 for more on the Deal Counter.

Shopping — checking off & removing items

The 🛒 Shop mode is where you spend most of your time at the store. Everything is designed to be quick to use with one hand.

Checking off an item

Tap the circle checkbox on the left of any item. It turns green with a checkmark, the item text gets a strikethrough, and the item slides to the bottom of the aisle — out of your way. The progress bar at the top fills up as you check more items off.

✅
All done? When every item is checked, the progress bar turns full green and you'll see "✅ All items checked!" — your cue to head to checkout.

Removing an item

Tap the ✕ button on the right side of any item to remove it from the list. You don't need to be in Edit mode to do this.

Clearing all checked items at once

Once you've checked some items, a Clear checked button appears next to the progress bar. Tap it to remove everything that's been checked in one go — useful when you want to reuse your aisle layout for the next trip.

Collapsing aisles

Tap the ⌄ chevron on any aisle header to collapse it and hide its items. Tap again (it becomes ›) to expand. Great for hiding aisles you've already finished.

Starting a new trip

Tap the ✨ button (top right, or the floating button on mobile). You'll get two choices:

  • 🔄 Keep aisles, clear all items — removes every item but keeps your aisle layout intact. Best for a regular weekly shop.
  • 📄 Start completely fresh — resets aisles and items back to the defaults for that store.

On Sale labels

⚙️
Needs to be turned on first Tap the ⚙️ button → Settings → turn on 🏷️ On Sale labels.

When On Sale labels are enabled, each item on your list gets a small sale? button on the right side.

1
When you spot something on sale, tap sale? on that item.
2
It flips to a bold red SALE badge, and the item row gets a subtle red tint so it stands out on your list.
3
Tap it again to clear the sale tag.
🏷️
On your printout Items marked as SALE show a small red "SALE" badge on the printed list too.

⭐

Deal Counter

The Deal Counter tracks promotions like "5 for $5" or "Buy 10, mix and match" — where you need to grab a certain number of qualifying items to get a discount.

⚙️
Setting it up Tap ⚙️ → Settings → turn on ⭐ Deal counter. Then set the deal name (e.g. 5 for $5) and the number of items needed using the − and + buttons.

Using the counter while shopping

1
When you pick up an item that qualifies for the deal, tap the ⭐ button on that item. It turns orange to show it's being counted.
2
A bar appears at the bottom of the screen showing your progress: "3 more items to complete the deal."
3
When you hit the target, it turns green: "✅ 1 × 5 for $5 deal in cart!"
4
Keep going! If you want two deals (e.g. 10 items for a 5-for-$5), just keep tapping ⭐. The counter tracks multiples: "✅ 2 complete + 3/5 toward another deal."
⭐
Quantity counts! If an item has a quantity (e.g. 6 cans), tapping ⭐ on it adds 6 to the deal count — not just 1. So grabbing a 6-pack counts as 6 toward your deal automatically.
💡
Checked items don't count Once you check an item off, it's no longer counted in the deal total. The counter always reflects what's still in your cart.

Saving lists to history

You can save a snapshot of your current list at any time. The app holds your last 10 saved lists across all stores.

Saving a list

1
Tap the 💾 button at the top.
2
Give the list a name — something like Weekly Shop – April 5 or Holiday Baking.
3
Tap 💾 Save. The button briefly shows ✅ to confirm it worked.

Loading a saved list

1
Tap the 📋 button. A badge shows how many lists you have saved.
2
Browse your saved lists. You'll see the store name, date saved, item count, and a preview of which aisles had items.
3
Tap Load next to any list. A popup asks which store to load it into — pick the one you want.
💡
Great for regular trips Save your usual weekly list once. At the start of each week, load it back in, add anything new, and you're ready to go.

Printing or saving as PDF

Tap the 🖨️ button to print your list. The app will produce a clean, formatted version with no buttons, checkboxes, or app chrome — just your list.

The printout includes:

  • Store name and icon at the top
  • Your list name in large, readable text
  • The date and time printed, plus item count
  • Each aisle as a labeled section with empty checkboxes to tick by hand
  • Quantities, and a SALE badge on any items you marked as on sale
  • Deal items marked with a ⭐ if the deal counter is active

Saving as a PDF instead of printing

1
Tap 🖨️. Your browser's print dialog opens.
2
In the Destination or Printer dropdown, choose Save as PDF (the exact label varies by browser).
3
Click Save and choose where to put the PDF file. You can then share it, email it, or open it on your phone.
📱
On a phone or tablet The "Save as PDF" option may appear differently. On iPhone/iPad, look for Share → Print → Pinch out to zoom → Share as PDF. On Android, choose Save as PDF in the print dialog.

Backup & restore your data

Your data lives in the browser — which means it's safe as long as you use the same browser on the same computer. To protect against losing everything, use the Backup feature to save a copy of your data to a regular file.

⚠️
Your data may be lost if you…
  • Clear your browser history or cache
  • Open the file in a different browser (e.g. switching from Chrome to Safari)
  • Open the file on a different computer
  • Your browser decides to clear storage for the file

Exporting a backup

1
Tap ⚙️ at the top of the screen to open the settings panel.
2
Tap Download Backup File. A file named something like grocery-list-backup-2025-04-05.json will download.
3
Save it somewhere safe — a USB drive, your cloud storage (iCloud, Google Drive, Dropbox), or email it to yourself.

Restoring from a backup

1
Tap ⚙️ to open settings.
2
Tap Choose Backup File… and select your saved backup file.
3
Confirm the warning (this replaces all current data). Your stores, aisles, items, saved lists, and settings will all be restored.
📅
Good habit: back up monthly Once a month, export a backup and save it somewhere outside the browser. Takes 10 seconds and gives you peace of mind.

Quick reference — every button explained

🌙 / ☀️ Dark / Light mode Switches the color theme. Remembers your choice.
📋 Saved Lists View, load, or delete your saved list history.
💾 Save List Save a named snapshot of the current list.
🖨️ Print / PDF Prints a clean version. Choose "Save as PDF" in the print dialog.
⚙️ Settings & Backup Backup, restore, Sale labels, and Deal Counter settings.
✨ New List Clear items or fully reset the current store.
🛒 Shop Shopping mode Check items off, mark sales, count deal items.
✏️ Edit Edit mode Manage stores, aisles, and their order.
+ Store Add store Add a new store (up to 5 total).
▲ ▼ Reorder aisles Move an aisle up or down. Only visible in Edit mode.
⌄ / › Collapse aisle Hide or show an aisle's items.
sale? On Sale tag Mark an item as on sale. Turns bold red when active.
⭐ Deal item Counts this item toward your deal promotion. Turns orange when active.
✕ Remove item Deletes the item from the list. Works in any mode.
🙋
Something not working? Try refreshing the browser page (press F5 or pull down on mobile). Your data is safe — it's saved automatically and a refresh won't lose anything.
